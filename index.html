About NotesAA

NotesAA is built to make studying less messy and more organized. Instead of having notes scattered everywhere, it keeps everything in one place, making it easier to find what you need. It’s simple by design — no unnecessary features, no confusing layout, just a clean space to focus. Created by a student, it understands how chaotic school can get. It’s made for every student — even that one friend who studies the night before and still acts surprised
